## Cross-cutting takeaways
- **Hottest single live signal:** SIEM hiring posts (ZainTECH-type Managed SOCs) and Legal "which-tools" re-evaluations (Hanson Bridgett) — both are accounts publicly broadcasting the exact pain.
- **Build-vs-partner verdict across the 6:** 5 of 6 are PARTNER/INTEGRATE plays (the tool exists; you deliver migration + integration + client workflow + governance). **Low-code (#5) is the one true BUILD play** — best fit for a done-for-you engineering agency's core competency.
- **Reference vs lead discipline:** the ⚪ "already switched" names are your *proof slides and persona maps*, not your call list. The hunt targets are the ✅/◻ profiles still living the pain.
